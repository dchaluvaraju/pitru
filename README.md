# Pitṛ

## पितृ

---

## Symbolic Definition

**Pitṛ** is not merely “father.”  
It is the **Sustaining Intelligence**.

From:
- √pā — to protect, to preserve, to nourish  
- -tṛ — the agent, the one who acts  

Pitṛ = **The Agent of Protection**

---

## Metaphysical Interpretation

Where form has emerged,  
Pitṛ preserves it.

Where structure exists,  
Pitṛ stabilizes it.

Where life unfolds,  
Pitṛ sustains continuity.

Pitṛ is the principle that converts:
- Structure → Stability  
- Creation → Preservation  
- Emergence → Order  

---

## Archetypal Layer

- Protector  
- Sustainer  
- Law-Giver  
- Structural Guardian  

---

## Essence

> Pitṛ is the intelligence that sustains form once it has been born.
